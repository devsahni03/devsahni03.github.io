Approach

Built a 60,000-row synthetic loan portfolio (loan type, region, credit score, DPD bucket, outstanding principal, collections) modeled on realistic Indian NBFC/bank risk patterns — default probability driven primarily by credit score with loan-type and regional modifiers. Loaded into SQLite and analyzed with SQL using multi-table joins, subqueries, and aggregate functions to compute NPA rate by segment, a CASE-based credit score risk curve, regional collection efficiency, vintage trend, and a benchmark-based recovery opportunity estimate. Reproduced and visualized in Python (pandas + matplotlib); DAX measures mirror the same logic in Power BI.
